Join us this fall at the 2024 edition of the Mogollon Monster!

Choose from the point to point 100 mile or get a hefty dose of the Monster in the 42k ‚ÄúMonster Marathon‚Äù. No matter which distance you choose, you are sure to appreciate breathtaking climbs up the Rim, exhilarating descents down, spectacular views in between, and possible monster sightings at each turn.

Featuring rugged terrain, an altitude in excess of one mile above sea level and, on occasion, severe weather, it‚Äôs challenging, fun and offers a number of running routes for runners of all skill and fitness levels, including a marathon, half marathon, 10k and new this year a 5K and .30 Mile High Fun Run.
Proceeds from this race will go directly into the scholarship fund at the James Family Prescott YMCA. This fund provides families in financial need to participate in childcare, swimming lessons, youth sports and gymnastics classes. Your participation in this race not only promotes your personal health, but also promotes the overall health of the Prescott Community.

Join runners from all over the country in this prestigious event ‚Äì and show your friends and family that you are ‚ÄúTough Enough‚Äù.

Race Times:
Marathon- 6:00am
Half Marathon- 7:00am
10K- 7:30am
5K- 8:00am
